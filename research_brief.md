## Value-add / refi-out lens
User preference: prioritize value-add opportunities where acquisition + rehab + refinance can recover most or all invested cash.

Read on this asset:
- Not an obvious value-add / all-cash-back-refi candidate at the current ask.
- Looks closer to a marketed operating hospitality asset than a discounted forced-appreciation setup.
- There may be operational upside from the newly added Stargazer unit, but that is different from buying below replacement / below stabilized value and refinancing out quickly.
- Likely too much equity trapped for a strict BRRRR / refi-out discipline unless basis comes down substantially or financials are much stronger than public marketing suggests.

## Recommendation direction
