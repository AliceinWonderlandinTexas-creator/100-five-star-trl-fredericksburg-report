Key Diligence Items

  • Confirm exactly what conveys: parcel count, acreage, and whether all cabins / barndo / flex building are included.
  • Get a true structure schedule: sf, bed/bath count, use, utilities, and permits for each building.
  • Verify trailing 12-month financials by unit: ADR, occupancy, gross revenue, owner blocks.
  • Verify expenses: utilities, OTA fees, cleaning, maintenance, hot tubs, insurance, septic / water systems.
  • Confirm situs and STR framework: city, ETJ, or county-only; HOT burden; compliance of all rentable units.
  • Check lender appetite: residential STR vs specialty / quasi-commercial underwriting.
